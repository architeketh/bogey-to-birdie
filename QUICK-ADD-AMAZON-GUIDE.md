### **What's an ASIN?**

ASIN = Amazon Standard Identification Number

- **It's the unique 10-character product code**
- Format: B084RNGWQJ (starts with B followed by 9 alphanumeric chars)
- Every Amazon product has one
- **Example:** In `https://www.amazon.com/dp/B084RNGWQJ`, the ASIN is `B084RNGWQJ`

The tool finds this automatically and creates a clean affiliate link!
